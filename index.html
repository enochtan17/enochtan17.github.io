Projects

Zing

Zing is a web app for organized community inspired by Slack. It was made with React/Redux, JavaScript, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Websockets. A highlight is my implementation of editing capabilities in modal designs. I used Redux to accomplish this, providing users with an elegant solution to make desired changes to workspace / channel names without clutter on the screen.

Middle Earth BBnB

A web app spun off of Airbnb, this site offers residents of the Middle Earth realm a superior experience, complete with 2nd breakfast (as indicated in the app name). It was made with React/Redux, JavaScript, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, and enhanced with Google Maps API. My proudest feature is the Google Maps API implementation, which I learned in the space of a day. The Airbnb feel of the web app would not be the same without it.
